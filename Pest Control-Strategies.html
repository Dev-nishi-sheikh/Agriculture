Pest Control Strategies

Organic Remedies

"Safe and eco-friendly solutions include using Neem oil to stop pests from feeding, applying bio-pesticides like Trichoderma, and using homemade garlic or soap sprays to repel insects without damaging the environment."

Emergency Care

"During severe outbreaks, take immediate action by applying targeted chemical sprays in expert-approved dosages. Quickly quarantine the field by removing and burning infected plants to prevent the disease from spreading."

IPM Methods

"IPM focuses on long-term prevention through a mix of techniques. It involves weekly crop monitoring, using pheromone traps to disrupt breeding, and selecting disease-resistant seed varieties to minimize chemical use."

Rotation

"Switch crop types every season to break the pest life cycle. This prevents specific insects from building up in the soil year after year."
